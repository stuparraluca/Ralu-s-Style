Noah's Arc Cluj

THE ASSOCIATION was established in 2003 at the initiative of Mrs. Bora Finuca. Until 2006 the activity of the association was supported by its financial efforts and its direct involvement. Since 2006 the activity of the association has intensified due to the increase of economic possibilities. Over time the activity has grown, due to the opportunities offered by the community.

Nuca Animal Welfare

NUCA is an apolitical and non-profit organization. Their strongest belief is that all animals have a right to respect. Their activity is entirely based on the work of a team of volunteers.
